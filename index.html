Home

Music is art. You probably have already listened this sentence before, and it’s true. Music is art because you can use it to express yourself or interpret the feelings that a composer had when creating a song. It’s important to provide support for those who want to engage in the wonderful world of music. That’s why we are here to provide a way for you to enjoy this experience as much as you can. In order to do that, you only need to know the basic of reading musical figures and tabs. You don’t know how to do that? No problem! Chords N’ Tabs will provide images to help you to learn the basics of music. You are an experienced musician? That’s nice! Chords N’ Tabs was made for you too, because we believe you have the right to have support to learn no matter how much you already know. As you will notice, in the world of music (such as almost everything) there will always be a lot of more interesting things to learn. And that’s why Chords N’ Tabs was created! To give you a helping hand from people who know what they are doing. After all, we know that if you are looking for our help to play new songs, it’s because you trust in us, and we won’t let you down.
